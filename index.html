Prop Desk Operations

📈 The Currency Rules

Welcome to the Prop Desk. Both players start with $1,000 in capital.

  • The Bet: Each round, you may wager any amount from $10 up to your current bankroll.
  • Time Limit: You have exactly 15 seconds per round to place your bet. If you do not commit in time, your current input (or the minimum ante) is automatically submitted.
  • Showdown Mechanics: The winner takes the loser's wager. Profits are capped at the winner's own wager amount (Effective Risk / Liquidity Matching).
  • Mandatory Ante: A minimum bet of $10 is required every round.
  • Margin Call: If either player's bankroll hits $0, they are liquidated.

📋 Hand Rankings (highest → lowest)

① Straight Flush3 same-suit sequential cards. Tie: higher top card. A-2-3 is lowest.
② Three of a Kind3 cards of same rank. Tie: higher rank wins.
③ Straight3 sequential cards, mixed suits. Tie: higher top card.
④ Flush3 same-suit cards, not sequential. Tie: top-down.
⑤ Pair2 cards same rank + kicker. Tie: pair, then kicker.
⑥ High CardNone of the above. Tie: compare cards top-down.
